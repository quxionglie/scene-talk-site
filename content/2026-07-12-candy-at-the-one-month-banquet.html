满月酒上的糖果

Candy at a Baby's One-Month Banquet

满月酒 Baby's one-month banquet糖果 Candy

参加完表舅家的满月酒后，父亲在家问孩子今天玩得开不开心

After a one-month banquet for a relative's baby, Dad asks the child if they had fun

Speakers
A 父亲 father|B 孩子 child
A: 今天玩得开心不？
jīn tiān wán dé kāi xīn bù?
Did you have fun today?
B: 你又没去！
nǐ yòu méi qù!
You weren't even there!
A: 难道我没去，你就不开心？
nán dào wǒ méi qù, nǐ jiù bù kāi xīn?
So you weren't happy just because I wasn't there?
B: 今天有好多糖果。
jīn tiān yǒu hǎo duō táng guǒ.
There was lots of candy today.
A: 你哥不是说在麻将馆，表舅给了很多葡萄吗？
nǐ gē bú shì shuō zài má jiāng guǎn, biǎo jiù gěi le hěn duō pú táo ma?
Didn't your brother say your uncle gave you lots of grapes at the mahjong parlor?
葡萄、花生、瓜子、糖果等都有
There were grapes, peanuts, sunflower seeds, candy, and more.
