冻胀的瓶子

The Swollen Bottle

冰箱 Freezer冰块 Ice

孩子发现放进冰箱一天的大水瓶冻成冰后胀起来了

The child finds the big bottle of water frozen solid and bulging after a day in the freezer

Speakers: A - 父亲 father|B - 孩子 child
B: 我的瓶子已经冻成冰了。
wǒ de píng zi yǐ jīng dòng chéng bīng le.
My bottle's frozen solid.
瓶子在冰箱里放了一天。
The bottle has been in the freezer for a day.
A: 拿出来看看。
ná chū lái kàn kàn.
Take it out. Let's see.
B: 已经涨起来了。
yǐ jīng zhǎng qǐ lái le.
It's bulging.
水变成冰后体积会增大。
Water expands when it freezes.
A: 下次瓶子水装少一点，不然就变成这样子了。
xià cì píng zi shuǐ zhuāng shǎo yì diǎn, bù rán jiù biàn chéng zhè yàng zi le.
Don't fill it up so much next time, or it'll end up like this.
A: 幸好是塑料的，不然就爆了。
xìng hǎo shì sù liào de, bù rán jiù bào le.
Good thing it's plastic, or it would've burst.
像罐装可乐放进冰箱，结冰后就可能会爆掉。
A can of Coke can burst if you leave it in the freezer and it freezes.
