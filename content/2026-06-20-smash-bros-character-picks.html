大乱斗选角色

Picking Characters in Smash Bros.

游戏 Games角色 Characters

父亲和两个孩子商量任天堂明星大乱斗每个人适合哪个角色

A father and two children talk about which Super Smash Bros. character each person should pick

A-父亲 fatherB-弟弟 younger brotherC-哥哥 older brother
A: 大乱斗选角色？小弟弟你要选哪个？
dà luàn dòu xuǎn jué sè? xiǎo dì dì nǐ yào xuǎn nǎ ge?
Are we picking Smash Bros. characters? Little guy, who do you want?
大乱斗 指的是 "任天堂明星大乱斗 特别版"
"Smash Bros." refers to Super Smash Bros. Ultimate.
B: 队长
duì zhǎng
Captain Falcon.
这里指游戏角色“飞隼队长”。
This refers to the game character Captain Falcon.
A: 哥哥你要选一八吗
gē gē nǐ yào xuǎn yī bā ma
What about you, big brother? Do you want Kazuya?
一八 Kazuya,铁拳系列角色。
Kazuya is a character from the Tekken series.
C: 嗯
en
Yeah.
A: 那我选 Joker 吧？
nà wǒ xuǎn Joker ba?
Then I'll go with Joker, okay?
B: 你要选林克
nǐ yào xuǎn lín kè
You should pick Link.
C: 林克很菜的
lín kè hěn cài de
Link isn't very good.
A: 林克会射箭，也很强的
lín kè huì shè jiàn, yě hěn qiáng de
Link can shoot arrows. He's strong too.
A: 你妈就选一个面面给她，长手怪
nǐ mā jiù xuǎn yí gè miàn miàn gěi tā, zhǎng shǒu guài
For Mom, let's give her Min Min, the long-armed one.
