不开空调

No Air Conditioner

空调 Air conditioning天气 Weather

家里其它房间都开了空调，只有父亲房间没开，孩子问父亲为什么不开

The AC is on in the other rooms, but not in Dad's room, so the child asks why he will not turn it on

Download PDF
A-父亲 fatherB-孩子 child
B: 老爸，你不开空调吗？天气很热了。
lǎo bà, nǐ bù kāi kōng tiáo ma? tiān qì hěn rè le.
Dad, are you not turning on the AC? It's getting really hot.
只是父亲自己的房间没开，其它房间都开了
Only Dad's room has no AC on; the other rooms do.
A: 不开不开我不开。
bù kāi bù kāi wǒ bù kāi.
No, no, I'm not turning it on.
父亲重复“不开”，带一点玩笑语气。
Dad repeats the line in a joking tone.
B: 妈妈没回来，谁来也不开。
mā mā méi huí lái, shuí lái yě bù kāi.
Mom's not back. No matter who comes, we won't turn it on.
儿歌《小白兔乖乖》 的歌词。孩子接着父亲的话唱的
The child follows Dad's line and borrows the pattern from the nursery rhyme "Xiao Bai Tu Guai Guai."
