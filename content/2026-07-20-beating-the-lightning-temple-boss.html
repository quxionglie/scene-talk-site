打赢雷之神殿的 Boss

Beating the Lightning Temple Boss

游戏 Gaming塞尔达传说 The Legend of Zelda

孩子打赢了雷之神殿的 Boss，并解释唯一一次失败是被小怪打死的。

The child beats the Lightning Temple boss after dying just once—to a regular enemy, not the boss.

Speakers: A - 父亲 father | B - 孩子 child
B: 我打赢了雷之神殿的 Boss。
wǒ dǎ yíng le léi zhī shén diàn de Boss.
I beat the Lightning Temple boss.
雷之神殿 Boss 是吉波得女王。
The Lightning Temple boss is Queen Gibdo.
A: 这么强！打了几次？
zhè me qiáng! dǎ le jǐ cì?
Wow, nice! How many tries did it take?
B: 死了一次，后面才打赢。我用了好多时间。
sǐ le yī cì, hòu miàn cái dǎ yíng. wǒ yòng le hǎo duō shí jiān.
I died once, but I finally beat it. It took me a long time.
B: 我就是被小怪扑过来打死的，不是 Boss 打死的！
wǒ jiù shì bèi xiǎo guài pū guò lái dǎ sǐ de, bú shì Boss dǎ sǐ de!
A regular enemy jumped at me and killed me. The boss didn't kill me!
A: 从后面偷袭的？
cóng hòu miàn tōu xí de?
Did it sneak up on you from behind?
B: 正面打的，我正在看上面的树，没注意到地面的情况。
zhèng miàn dǎ dī, wǒ zhèng zài kàn shàng miàn de shù, méi zhù yì dào dì miàn de qíng kuàng.
No, it came at me from the front. I was looking at the trees above me and wasn't watching the ground.
