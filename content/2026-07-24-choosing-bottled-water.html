选瓶装水

Choosing Bottled Water

买水 Buying water选择 Choosing

父亲问孩子要买多大瓶的水，孩子选了红色包装的水。

Dad asks what size bottle of water the child wants, and the child picks the one with red packaging.

Speakers: A - 父亲 father | B - 孩子 child
A: 你要买多大瓶的水？1升、2升，还是1.5升？
nǐ yào mǎi duō dà píng de shuǐ?1 shēng,2 shēng, hái shì 1.5 shēng?
What size water do you want? One liter, two liters, or 1.5?
B: 1.5升。
1.5 shēng.
1.5 liters.
B: 红色更健康。
hóng sè gèng jiàn kāng.
The red one is healthier.
矿泉水瓶外包装有很多种颜色。
The bottled water comes in packaging with several different colors.
