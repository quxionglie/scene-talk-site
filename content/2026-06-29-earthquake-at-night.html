昨晚地震

Last Night's Earthquake

地震 Earthquake安全 Safety

父亲和孩子们聊昨晚的地震和避险方法

A father and his children talk about last night's earthquake and earthquake safety

A-父亲 fatherB-孩子1 child 1C-孩子1/孩子2
A: 昨晚地震有感觉到吗？
zuó wǎn dì zhèn yǒu gǎn jué dào ma?
Did you feel the earthquake last night?
B: 没感觉。
méi gǎn jué.
No, I didn't feel it.
A: 宜宾5.5级，离我们不太远。
yí bīn 5.5 jí, lí wǒ men bù tài yuǎn.
It was a 5.5-magnitude quake in Yibin, not too far from us.
A: 我还起来观察了一下，再来就要跑下楼了。
wǒ hái qǐ lái guān chá le yī xià, zài lái jiù yào pǎo xià lóu le.
I even got up to check. If there was another shake, we would have had to run downstairs.
“再来”更像“再来一次/又震一下”
Here, "zài lái" means another shake or aftershock.
B: 不能坐电梯。
bù néng zuò diàn tī.
You can't take the elevator.
A: 我还说跑不了就躲在桌子下面呢。
wǒ hái shuō pǎo bù liǎo jiù duǒ zài zhuō zi xià miàn ne.
I was thinking, if we couldn't get out, we could hide under the table.
A: 你们老师有教过？
nǐ men lǎo shī yǒu jiào guò?
Did your teachers teach you about that?
C: 要躲在三角的地方。
yào duǒ zài sān jiǎo de dì fāng.
You should hide in a triangle spot.
三角:应该地震避险里说的“生命三角”。
Here, "triangle" refers to the "triangle of life" idea in earthquake safety.
