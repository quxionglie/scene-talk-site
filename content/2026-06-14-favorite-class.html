父亲问孩子最喜欢什么课，孩子说因为擅长所以喜欢数学

A father asks his child what class they like best, and the child says math because they are good at it

A-父亲 fatherB-孩子 child
A: 你最喜欢什么课？
nǐ zuì xǐ huān shén me kè?
What class do you like best?
B: 数学。
shù xué.
Math.
A: 为什么？
wèi shén me?
Why?
B: 因为我擅长。
yīn wèi wǒ shàn cháng.
Because I'm good at it.
A: 为什么不是体育呢？看来运动还是太少了。
wèi shén me bú shì tǐ yù ne? kàn lái yùn dòng hái shì tài shǎo le.
Why not PE? Looks like you still don't get enough exercise.

It includes offline content such as scene-based dialogues, key expressions, cultural tips, background articles, and human-recorded Chinese audio, helping you learn common, natural Chinese expressions used in real communication.

The iOS app is available on the App Store in all regions outside mainland China.
