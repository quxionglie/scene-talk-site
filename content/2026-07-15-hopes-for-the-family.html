对家人的希望

What He Wants from His Family

家庭 Family希望 Expectations

孩子希望妈妈温柔、弟弟听话，却对自己不做要求。

The child wants Mom to be gentle and his little brother to listen, but expects nothing of himself

Download PDF
A-父亲 fatherB-孩子 child
B: 我要温柔的妈妈！
wǒ yào wēn róu de mā mā!
I want Mom to be gentle!
A: 弟弟呢？
dì di ne?
What about your little brother?
B: 听话的弟弟。
tīng huà de dì di.
I want him to listen.
A: 你希望自己是什么样的哥哥？
nǐ xī wàng zì jǐ shì shén me yàng dī gē gē?
What kind of big brother do you want to be?
B: 没希望。
méi xī wàng.
Me? Nothing.
对自己不做要求
He doesn't expect anything of himself.
A: 不给自己一点希望吗，聪明的哥哥行不行？
bù gěi zì jǐ yì diǎn xī wàng ma, cōng míng dī gē gē xíng bù xíng?
Nothing for yourself? How about being a smart big brother?
