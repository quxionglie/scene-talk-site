岩石巨人怎么找到我

How Does the Stone Talus Find Me?

游戏 Gaming塞尔达传说 The Legend of Zelda

孩子好奇没有眼睛的岩石巨人怎么知道玩家在哪里。

The child wonders how the Stone Talus knows where the player is even though it has no eyes.

Speakers: A - 父亲 father | B - 孩子 child
B: 岩石巨人没有眼睛，怎么知道我在哪个方向？
yán shí jù rén méi yǒu yǎn jīng, zěn me zhī dào wǒ zài nǎ ge fāng xiàng?
The Stone Talus doesn't have eyes. How does it know where I am?
《塞尔达传说：王国之泪》的岩石巨人
The Stone Talus from The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom
A: 我也不知道。
wǒ yě bù zhī dào.
I don't know either.
B: 这就很奇怪。
zhè jiù hěn qí guài.
That's weird.
A: 我们研究一下。
wǒ men yán jiū yī xià.
Let's figure it out.
岩石巨人能通过魔力感知和地面震动判断林克(玩家)的位置。
The Stone Talus can locate Link (the player) by sensing magic and vibrations through the ground.
A: 你问了一个好问题！
nǐ wèn le yí gè hǎo wèn tí!
Good question!
