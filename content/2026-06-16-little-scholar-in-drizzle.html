毛毛雨里的小书生

A Little Scholar in the Drizzle

放学 After school下雨 Rain

毛毛雨里回家，孩子把书包顶在头上，又把“气”当成空气和力气。

On the way home in the drizzle, the child puts his backpack over his head and treats "qi" as both air and strength.

A-父亲 fatherB-孩子 child
B: 快走啊。
kuài zǒu a.
Come on, hurry up.
A: 下雨了，我帮你打伞。
xià yǔ le, wǒ bāng nǐ dǎ sǎn.
It's raining. I'll hold the umbrella for you.
B: 我自己来。
wǒ zì jǐ lái.
I've got it.
书包顶在自己头上。
He puts his backpack over his head.
A: 你这个样子像古代的小书生。
nǐ zhè ge yàng zi xiàng gǔ dài de xiǎo shū shēng.
You look like a little scholar from ancient China.
A: 你还能走吗？
nǐ hái néng zǒu ma?
Can you still walk?
走了一段路后。
After walking for a while.
B: 我没气了。
wǒ méi qì le.
I'm out of breath.
A: 你知道没气是什么意思吗？
nǐ zhī dào méi qì shì shén me yì si ma?
Do you know what "没气" means?
B: 没气就是没有力气了。
méi qì jiù shì méi yǒu lì qì le.
It means I don't have any energy left.
A: 那你还能走这么久？
nà nǐ hái néng zǒu zhè me jiǔ?
Then how did you walk this far?
B: 吸气。
xī qì.
I take a breath.
吸气的样子。孩子把“气”理解成空气，也当成力气来源。
The child treats "qi" as both air and a source of strength.
