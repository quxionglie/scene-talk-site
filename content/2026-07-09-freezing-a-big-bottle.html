冻一瓶冰块

Freezing a Big Bottle of Water

冰箱 Fridge降温 Cooling down

孩子想把大水瓶放进冰箱冻成冰块，用来避暑降温

The child wants to freeze a big bottle of water to cool down in the summer heat

Download PDF
A-父亲 fatherB-孩子 child
B: 冰箱中间两层是空的。
bīng xiāng zhōng jiān liǎng céng shì kōng de.
The middle two shelves in the freezer are empty.
A: 怎么今天你又想弄冰块？
zěn me jīn tiān nǐ yòu xiǎng nòng bīng kuài?
So you want to make ice again today?
B: 是啊。
shì a.
Yeah.
A: 又想学上次单子里的避暑降温？
yòu xiǎng xué shàng cì dān zi lǐ de bì shǔ jiàng wēn?
Are you doing that stay-cool thing from the handout again?
幼儿园打印的 A4 单子，问题之一：夏天有哪些方法避暑降温？
It was an A4 handout from kindergarten. One question asked how to stay cool in summer.
B: 是啊，我要降温。
shì a, wǒ yào jiàng wēn.
Yeah. I want to cool down.
A: 不要让你妈发现我们放了一个2kg的瓶子进去了啊？
bú yào ràng nǐ mā fā xiàn wǒ men fàng le yí gè 2kg de píng zi jìn qù le a?
Don't let your mom find out we put a two-kilo bottle in there, okay?
2kg 重的矿泉水瓶，大概放半天就会冻成冰块。
It is a two-kilo bottle of mineral water. It should freeze solid after about half a day.
