冻一瓶冰块

Freezing a Big Bottle of Water

冰箱 Freezer降温 Cooling down

孩子想把大水瓶放进冰箱冻成冰块，用来避暑降温

The child wants to freeze a big bottle of water to stay cool in summer

Speakers: A - 父亲 father | B - 孩子 child
B: 冰箱中间两层是空的。
bīng xiāng zhōng jiān liǎng céng shì kōng de.
The two middle shelves in the freezer are empty.
A: 怎么今天你又想弄冰块？
zěn me jīn tiān nǐ yòu xiǎng nòng bīng kuài?
Why do you want to make ice again today?
B: 是啊。
shì a.
Yeah.
A: 又想学上次单子里的避暑降温？
yòu xiǎng xué shàng cì dān zi lǐ de bì shǔ jiàng wēn?
Are you trying that stay-cool idea from the handout again?
幼儿园打印的 A4 单子，问题之一：夏天有哪些方法避暑降温？
It was an A4 handout from kindergarten. One of the questions was how to stay cool in summer.
B: 是啊，我要降温。
shì a, wǒ yào jiàng wēn.
Yeah. I want to cool off.
A: 不要让你妈发现我们放了一个2kg的瓶子进去了啊？
bú yào ràng nǐ mā fā xiàn wǒ men fàng le yí gè 2kg de píng zi jìn qù le a?
Don't let Mom find out we put a two-kilo bottle in there, okay?
2kg 重的矿泉水瓶，大概放半天就会冻成冰块。
It's a two-kilogram bottle of mineral water. It takes about half a day to freeze solid.
