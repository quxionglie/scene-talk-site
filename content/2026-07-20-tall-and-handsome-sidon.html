又帅又高的希多

Tall, Handsome Sidon

游戏 Gaming塞尔达传说 The Legend of Zelda

孩子和父亲聊起《塞尔达传说：王国之泪》里的角色，最后说自己最喜欢又帅又高的希多。

The child and his father talk about characters from The Legend of Zelda: Tears of the Kingdom. The child likes Sidon best because he's tall and handsome.

Speakers: A - 父亲 father | B - 孩子 child
B: 阿沅叫胖哥也行，因为阿沅的“沅”，肚子很圆就很胖。
ā yuán jiào pàng gē yě xíng, yīn wèi ā yuán de “yuán”, dǔ zi hěn yuán jiù hěn pàng.
You could call A-Yuan "Pang Ge" (Chubby Guy), too. The "Yuan" in his name sounds like "yuan" (round), and his round belly makes him chubby.
“沅”和“圆”同音。
The Chinese characters “沅” and “圆” are both pronounced “yuan.”
A: 是吗？
shì ma?
Really?
B: 嗯，希多吸的是水。
en, xī duō xī de shì shuǐ.
Yeah. The "Xi" in Xi-Duo sounds like "xi" (suck). He sucks up water.
“希”和“吸”同音。
The Chinese characters “希” and “吸” are both pronounced “xi.”
A: 那露珠呢？
nà lù zhū ne?
What about Riju?
B: 露珠，嗯？我想知道小鸟的名字叫什么？
lù zhū,en? wǒ xiǎng zhī dào xiǎo niǎo de míng zì jiào shén me?
Riju... hmm? I wonder what the little bird's name is.
小鸟在游戏里叫丘栗，但“栗”很多人不会读。
The bird's Chinese name is Qiu-Li, but many people don't know how to pronounce “栗” (“Li”).
A: 你看一下游戏里的名人录，反正名字比较难念。
nǐ kàn yī xià yóu xì lǐ de míng rén lù, fǎn zhèng míng zì bǐ jiào nán niàn.
Check the Character Profiles in the game. His name's pretty hard to pronounce anyway.
A: 你是喜欢小鸟还是胖子？
nǐ shì xǐ huān xiǎo niǎo hái shì pàng zi?
Do you like the bird or the fat guy?
B: 我喜欢希多！
wǒ xǐ huān xī duō!
I like Sidon!
A: 为什么？
wèi shén me?
Why?
B: 希多长得又帅又高！
xī duō zhǎng dé yòu shuài yòu gāo!
Sidon's tall and handsome!
