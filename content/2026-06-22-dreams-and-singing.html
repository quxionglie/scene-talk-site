梦里的唱歌

Singing in a Dream

家庭 Family梦 Dreams

孩子说梦到自己唱歌，但不知道梦是怎么来的

A child says they dreamed about singing, but they do not know where dreams come from

Speakers: A - 父亲 father | B - 孩子 child
A: 昨天梦到了什么？
zuó tiān mèng dào le shén me?
What did you dream about last night?
B: 梦到我在唱歌，还有一个忘了
mèng dào wǒ zài chàng gē, hái yǒu yí gè wàng le
I dreamed I was singing. There was another one too, but I forgot it.
A: 你觉得今天还会做梦吗？
nǐ jué de jīn tiān hái huì zuò mèng ma?
Do you think you'll dream again tonight?
B: 会
huì
Yes.
A: 梦是怎么来的，你知道不？
mèng shì zěn me lái de, nǐ zhī dào bù?
Do you know where dreams come from?
B: 嗯，不知道
en, bù zhī dào
Uh... I don't know.
