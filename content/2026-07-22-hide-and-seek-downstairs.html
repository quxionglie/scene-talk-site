楼下捉迷藏

Hide-and-Seek Downstairs

捉迷藏 Hide-and-seek游戏 Games

孩子在小区楼下和伙伴商量玩捉迷藏。

Downstairs in the compound, the child and another kid talk about playing hide-and-seek.

Speakers: A - 孩子 child | B - 姐姐 older kid
A: 我们来玩捉迷藏吧？
wǒ men lái wán zhuō mí cáng ba?
Want to play hide-and-seek?
B: 可以！
kě yǐ!
Sure!
B: 我们先来石头剪刀布！
wǒ men xiān lái shí tou jiǎn dāo bù!
Let's do rock-paper-scissors first!
A: 谁输了谁就来找，谁赢了谁就来藏。
shuí shū le shuí jiù lái zhǎo, shuí yíng le shuí jiù lái cáng.
Loser seeks, winner hides.
