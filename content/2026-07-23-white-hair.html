头上的白毛

A Gray Hair

白头发 White hair家庭 Family

孩子发现父亲头上有白头发，父亲感叹自己老了。

The child notices a gray hair on Dad's head, and Dad says he is getting old.

Speakers: A - 父亲 father | B - 孩子 child
B: 爸爸，你头上有白毛了！
bà bà, nǐ tóu shàng yǒu bái máo le!
Dad, you've got a gray hair!
“白毛”这里是“白头发”的意思。容易让人联想到电影《古惑仔》里的“白毛/黄毛”(不良少年)。
Here, "bai mao" means a gray or white hair, but it can also bring to mind "White Hair" or "Yellow Hair," nicknames for street-punk types in Young and Dangerous.
A: 是吗？
shì ma?
Really?
B: 还满长的，但还是黑色的多。
hái mǎn zhǎng de, dàn hái shì hēi sè de duō.
It's pretty long, but you still have way more black hair.
A: 老了，你们让我省心点就好。
lǎo le, nǐ men ràng wǒ shěng xīn diǎn jiù hǎo.
I'm getting old. Just don't make me worry so much.
