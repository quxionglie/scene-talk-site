到处都是“胖哥”

"Pang Ge" Everywhere

街边小店 Local Shops招牌 Signs

父子出门看到两家招牌带“胖哥”的小店，想起昨天刚聊过的游戏角色阿沅。

A father and child see two small shops with "Pang Ge" in their names and think of Yunobo, the game character they talked about yesterday.

Speakers: A - 父亲 father | B - 孩子 child
A: 看，又是胖哥。
kàn, yòu shì pàng gē.
Look, another "Pang Ge" (Chubby Guy).
B: 好多胖哥啊！
hǎo duō pàng gē a!
So many "Pang Ge" shops!
A: 昨天刚说到胖哥，今天出来都是胖哥。
zuó tiān gāng shuō dào pàng gē, jīn tiān chū lái dōu shì pàng gē.
We just talked about "Pang Ge" yesterday, and today we're seeing it everywhere.
“胖哥”昨天指游戏里的阿沅，现在指小店招牌上的名字。
Yesterday, "Pang Ge" referred to Yunobo in the game. Today, it's part of the names on two small shop signs.
A: “胖哥副食”，“胖哥大碗面”。
“pàng gē fù shí”,“pàng gē dà wǎn miàn”.
"Pang Ge Grocery." "Pang Ge Big Bowl Noodles."
“胖哥”都是小店招牌上的名字。
Both are names on signs for small local shops.
