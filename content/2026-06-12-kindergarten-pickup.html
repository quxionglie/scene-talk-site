幼儿园 Kindergarten

父亲、孩子在放学路上聊天

A father and child talk on the way home from kindergarten

Speakers: A - 父亲 father | B - 孩子 child
A: 今天是星期几啊？
jīn tiān shì xīng qī jǐ a?
What day is it today?
B: 星期五。
xīng qī wǔ.
It's Friday.
A: 你为什么背一个枕头出来啊？
nǐ wèi shén me bèi yí gè zhěn tou chū lái a?
Why did you bring your pillow home today?
B: 要拿回去洗！还有一个单子，下周要交。
yào ná huí qù xǐ! hái yǒu yí gè dān zi, xià zhōu yào jiāo.
I have to take it home and wash it! There's also a form we need to bring back next week.
A: 是要你妈填？
shì yào nǐ mā tián?
Is your mom supposed to fill it out?
B: 嗯。
en.
Mm-hmm.
A: 那回去再看看。
nà huí qù zài kàn kàn.
Okay, we'll check it when we get home.
