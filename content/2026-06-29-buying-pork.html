买猪肉

Buying Pork

买菜 Grocery shopping付款 Payment

顾客在菜市场买一斤猪肉

A customer buys one jin of pork at a market

A-顾客 customerB-老板 vendor
A: 老板，猪肉怎么卖？
lǎo bǎn, zhū ròu zěn me mài?
Boss, how much is the pork?
在中国购物时，“老板”是最常见、最接地气的店主尊称。
In China, "boss" is a very common, down-to-earth way to address a shop owner or vendor.
B: 猪肉12元一斤。
zhū ròu 12 yuán yī jīn.
It's 12 yuan per jin.
A: 那给我称一斤。
nà gěi wǒ chēng yī jīn.
One jin, please.
B: 好的。
hǎo de.
Sure.
