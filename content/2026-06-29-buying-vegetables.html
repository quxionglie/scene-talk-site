Buying Vegetables

买菜 Grocery shopping付款 Payment

顾客在菜市场买茄子、苦瓜、丝瓜和豇豆

A customer buys eggplant, bitter melon, loofah, and long beans at a market

A-顾客 customerB-阿姨 vendor
A: 阿姨，这个茄子、苦瓜、丝瓜、豇豆都怎么卖的？
ā yí, zhè ge qié zi, kǔ guā, sī guā, jiāng dòu dōu zěn me mài de?
Auntie, how much are the eggplant, bitter melon, loofah, and long beans?
豇豆，学名 Vigna unguiculata。
Long bean, scientific name Vigna unguiculata.
B: 茄子和豇豆还有丝瓜是3元一斤，苦瓜要贵点4元一斤。
qié zi hé jiāng dòu hái yǒu sī guā shì 3 yuán yī jīn, kǔ guā yào guì diǎn 4 yuán yī jīn.
Eggplant, long beans, and loofah are 3 yuan per jin. Bitter melon is a bit more, 4 yuan per jin.
A: 那请你每样帮我称一斤，看看一共多少钱？
nà qǐng nǐ měi yàng bāng wǒ chēng yī jīn, kàn kàn yī gòng duō shǎo qián?
Then one jin of each, please. How much is that total?
B: 总共13块，给现金还是扫码支付？
zǒng gòng 13 kuài, gěi xiàn jīn hái shì sǎo mǎ zhī fù?
13 yuan total. Cash or scan to pay?
A: 扫码吧，谢谢阿姨。
sǎo mǎ ba, xiè xiè ā yí.
I'll scan. Thanks, Auntie.
B: 不客气。
bú kè qì.
No problem.
