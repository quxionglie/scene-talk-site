肯德基取餐

Picking Up an Order at KFC

肯德基 KFC堂食 Dine-in取餐 Order Pickup

父亲在肯德基前台确认，服务员递上冰激凌并补拿套餐里的书

The father checks on his order at the KFC counter. The employee gives him his ice cream, then gets the book that comes with the combo

Speakers
A 父亲 father|B 服务员 KFC employee
A: 92 号好了吗？
92 hào hǎo le ma?
Is order 92 ready?
有时别人 93/94 已经好了，而你的还在准备中，就可以去前台确认一下
Sometimes orders 93 and 94 are already ready while yours still says "Preparing." You can check at the counter if that happens.
B: 好了，这是你的。冰激凌现在就要吗？
hǎo le, zhè shì nǐ de. bīng jī líng xiàn zài jiù yào ma?
It's ready. Here you go. Would you like your ice cream now?
冰激凌容易化掉，似乎可以不用立刻拿走。
Since ice cream melts quickly, it seems you can wait and pick it up later.
A: 是的。
shì de.
Yes, please.
B: 这是你的冰激凌。
zhè shì nǐ de bīng jī líng.
Here's your ice cream.
A: 好的，谢谢！
hǎo de, xiè xiè!
Okay, thanks!
B: 套餐里还有一本书，你们拿了吗？
tào cān lǐ hái yǒu yī běn shū, nǐ men ná le ma?
There's also a book with your combo. Did you get one?
儿童套餐里有一本书，随机发的。
The book that comes with the kids' meal is chosen at random.
A: 没有！
méi yǒu!
No, we didn't!
B: 稍等，我帮你拿一下！
shāo děng, wǒ bāng nǐ ná yī xià!
Just a moment. I'll get it for you.
