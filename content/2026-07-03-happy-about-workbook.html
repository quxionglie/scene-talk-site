高兴的事

Something to Be Happy About

放假 School break练习册 Workbook

父亲想听点高兴事，孩子却高兴妈妈买了练习册，让父亲哭笑不得

The father asks for something happy, but the child's answer is a new workbook from Mom, leaving him unsure whether to laugh or cry

Speakers: A - 父亲 father | B - 孩子 child
A: 天天听《伤心太平洋》，不说伤心的事了，你有高兴的事吗？
tiān tiān tīng 《 shāng xīn tài píng yáng 》, bù shuō shāng xīn de shì le, nǐ yǒu gāo xìng de shì ma?
We've been listening to "The Sad Pacific" every day. Enough sad stuff. Do you have anything happy to tell me?
《伤心太平洋》是任贤齐唱的歌。
"Shang Xin Tai Ping Yang" ("The Sad Pacific") is a song by Richie Jen.
B: 妈妈给我买了练习册。
mā mā gěi wǒ mǎi le liàn xí cè.
Mom bought me a workbook.
A: 放假了别人都是天天想玩，你还想做题！
fàng jià le bié rén dōu shì tiān tiān xiǎng wán, nǐ hái xiǎng zuò tí!
It's school break. Other kids just want to play all day, but you still want to do workbook problems!
A: 我眼泪都要出来了，你说我是想哭还是想笑？
wǒ yǎn lèi dōu yào chū lái le, nǐ shuō wǒ shì xiǎng kū hái shì xiǎng xiào?
I'm about to tear up. Am I trying to cry or laugh here?
父亲哭笑不得
The father does not know whether to laugh or cry.
B: 想哭？
xiǎng kū?
Cry?
