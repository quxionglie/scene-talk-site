第一名和奖牌

First Place and Medals

家庭 Family名次 Rankings

父亲和孩子聊第一第二

A father and child talk about first and second place

A-父亲 fatherB-孩子 child
B: 第一名是冠军，第二名是亚军
dì yì míng shì guàn jūn, dì èr míng shì yà jūn
First place is the champion. Second place is the runner-up.
A: 冠军得金牌，亚军是什么牌?
guàn jūn dé jīn pái, yà jūn shì shén me pái?
The champion gets a gold medal. What medal does the runner-up get?
B: 不知道
bù zhī dào
I don't know.
A: 银牌
yín pái
A silver medal.
A: 古代考试第一名叫状元,第二名呢？
gǔ dài kǎo shì dì yì míng jiào zhuàng yuán, dì èr míng ne?
In the old imperial exams, first place was called zhuangyuan. What about second place?
B: 不知道
bù zhī dào
I don't know.
A: 第二名榜眼，第三名探花
dì èr míng bǎng yǎn, dì sān míng tàn huā
Second place was bangyan, and third place was tanhua.
A: 英文里第一叫什么？
yīng wén lǐ dì yī jiào shén me?
How do you say "di yi" in English?
B: one
one
One.
A: 不对，第一是 first, 第二是 second, 不是 one two
bú duì, dì yī shì first, dì èr shì second, bú shì one two
Not quite. "Di yi" is first, and "di er" is second, not one and two.
