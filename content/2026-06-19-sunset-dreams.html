夕阳下的梦想

Sunset Dreams

夕阳 Sunset梦想 Dreams

父亲和两个孩子看夕阳聊天，孩子们说起钱、游戏世界和剑，父亲说自己想退休喝茶。

A father and two children chat while watching the sunset. The children talk about money, a game world, and a sword, and the father says he wants to retire and drink tea.

A-父亲 fatherB-孩子1 child 1C-孩子2 child 2
A: 刚好太阳要下山，看一下夕阳聊聊天。
gāng hǎo tài yáng yào xià shān, kàn yī xià xī yáng liáo liáo tiān.
The sun is about to set. Let's watch the sunset and talk for a bit.
A: 你们以后有什么梦想不？
nǐ men yǐ hòu yǒu shén me mèng xiǎng bù?
Do you two have any dreams for the future?
B: 没什么梦想，就希望钱多。
méi shén me mèng xiǎng, jiù xī wàng qián duō.
Not really. I just want a lot of money.
A: 你呢？
nǐ ne?
What about you?
C: 没有。
méi yǒu.
Nothing.
A: 或者最想做的事也行。
huò zhě zuì xiǎng zuò de shì yě xíng.
Or just something you really want to do.
C: 就想买一把剑，进入游戏世界。
jiù xiǎng mǎi yī bǎ jiàn, jìn rù yóu xì shì jiè.
I just want to buy a sword and enter a game world.
A: 我现在就想退休了，喝喝茶。
wǒ xiàn zài jiù xiǎng tuì xiū le, hē hē chá.
Right now, I just want to retire and drink tea.
