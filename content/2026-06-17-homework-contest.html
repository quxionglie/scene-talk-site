Homework Contest

作业 Homework比赛 Contest

父亲问孩子作业比赛怎么比，孩子说都是做过的题目。

A father asks how the homework contest worked, and the child says all the questions were ones they had done before.

Speakers
A 父亲 father|B 孩子 child
A: 听说你们今天有作业比赛？
tīng shuō nǐ men jīn tiān yǒu zuò yè bǐ sài?
I heard you had a homework contest today.
小学作业比赛
An elementary school homework contest
B: 是啊，每人发一张试卷。
shì a, měi rén fā yī zhāng shì juàn.
Yeah. Each person got a test paper.
A: 怎么比的？
zěn me bǐ de?
How did it work?
B: 随机发一套试卷，60分钟做完，试卷可能是语文、数学或英语。
suí jī fā yī tào shì juàn,60 fēn zhōng zuò wán, shì juàn kě néng shì yǔ wén, shù xué huò yīng yǔ.
We each got a random paper and had 60 minutes to finish it. It could be Chinese, math, or English.
A: 比赛有奖励不？
bǐ sài yǒu jiǎng lì bù?
Was there a prize?
B: 考100分就有。
kǎo 100 fēn jiù yǒu.
You get one if you score 100.
A: 感觉有点难度啊。
gǎn jué yǒu diǎn nán dù a.
That sounds a little hard.
B: 简单，都是做过的题目。
jiǎn dān, dōu shì zuò guò de tí mù.
It was easy. They were all questions we'd done before.
