暑假去划船

Summer Kayaking

暑假 Summer vacation划船 Kayaking

孩子想让暑假更美好，父亲提议一起去划皮划艇

The child wants to make summer vacation even better, so Dad suggests going kayaking together

Download PDF
A-父亲 fatherB-孩子 child
B: 我要让暑假过得更美好。
wǒ yào ràng shǔ jià guò dé gèng měi hǎo.
I want to make summer vacation even better.
A: 那明天去不去划船吗？
nà míng tiān qù bù qù huá chuán ma?
How about we go kayaking tomorrow, then?
B: 去。
qù.
Yeah!
A: 千万不要掉到湖里。上次我都怕你掉进水里了。
qiān wàn bú yào diào dào hú lǐ. shàng cì wǒ dōu pà nǐ diào jìn shuǐ lǐ le.
Make sure you don't fall in the lake. Last time, I was worried you might fall in.
A: 等一下问问队长去不去?
děng yí xià wèn wèn duì zhǎng qù bù qù?
Let's ask Captain later if he wants to go.
称呼为"队长"是因为“大乱斗选角色”选了队长，而"老板"已经退休了。前面的对话都有提到。
They call the younger brother "Captain" because he picked Captain Falcon in the earlier "Picking Characters in Smash Bros." dialogue. "Boss" had already retired in another dialogue.
A: 我们坐三人皮划艇，弟弟坐前面，我坐中间，你坐后面。
wǒ men zuò sān rén pí huá tǐng, dì di zuò qián miàn, wǒ zuò zhōng jiān, nǐ zuò hòu miàn.
We'll get a three-person kayak. Your brother can sit in front, I'll sit in the middle, and you can sit in the back.
三人皮划艇价格大概130元/时。事实上成人应该坐最后，避免出现意外情况。
A three-person kayak costs about 130 yuan an hour. For safety, the adult should actually sit in the back.
