晚上开空调吗

Air Conditioner Tonight?

骑车 Biking空调 Air conditioning

父亲问孩子今天骑车热不热，晚上要不要开空调

A father asks if the child rode today, if it was hot, and whether to turn on the air conditioner tonight

Speakers: A - 父亲 father | B - 孩子 child
A: 你今天骑车了吗？
nǐ jīn tiān qí chē le ma?
Did you ride your bike today?
在小区里骑自行车
The child was riding in the residential community.
B: 早上骑了，下午没骑。
zǎo shàng qí le, xià wǔ méi qí.
In the morning. Not in the afternoon.
A: 热吗？
rè ma?
Was it hot?
B: 热呀。
rè ya.
Yeah, hot.
A: 出汗了吧？
chū hàn le ba?
You got sweaty, right?
B: 很多汗。
hěn duō hàn.
A lot.
A: 今晚要开空调吗？
jīn wǎn yào kāi kōng tiáo ma?
Should we turn on the air conditioner tonight?
近期气温 22~30 度左右，所以没有给孩子晚上开空调。
Temperatures have been around 22-30°C lately, so they have not turned on the air conditioner for the child at night.
B: （不说话）
（ bù shuō huà ）
(Silence.)
