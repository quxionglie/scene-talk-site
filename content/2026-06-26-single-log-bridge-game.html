独木桥游戏

The Single-Log Bridge Game

游戏 Game

孩子提议玩独木桥游戏，父亲解释独木桥和独木舟的意思

A child asks Dad to play the single-log bridge game, and Dad explains what a single-log bridge and a dugout canoe are

A-父亲 fatherB-孩子 child
B: 爸爸，来玩独木桥游戏!
bà bà, lái wán dú mù qiáo yóu xì!
Dad, come play the single-log bridge game!
独木桥游戏：一个人坐在椅子上，脚靠在床上，小孩从脚上走过。
In this game, one person sits on a chair with one foot on the bed, and the child walks across that foot.
A: 你们见过独木桥吗？
nǐ men jiàn guò dú mù qiáo ma?
Have you ever seen a single-log bridge?
B: 没有
méi yǒu
No.
A: 就是一根木头组成的桥
jiù shì yī gēn mù tou zǔ chéng de qiáo
It's a bridge made from a single log.
A: 独木舟呢？
dú mù zhōu ne?
What about a dugout canoe?
B: 不知道
bù zhī dào
I don't know.
A: 就是一根木头中间被挖空，做成的小船
jiù shì yī gēn mù tou zhōng jiān bèi wā kōng, zuò chéng de xiǎo chuán
It's a little boat made by hollowing out a single log.
A: 所以一只脚才是独木桥。我给你们看独木桥图片
suǒ yǐ yī zhī jiǎo cái shì dú mù qiáo. wǒ gěi nǐ men kàn dú mù qiáo tú piàn
So one foot is the single-log bridge. Let me show you a picture of one.
B: 走过去不会断吗?
zǒu guò qù bú huì duàn ma?
Wouldn't it break if you walked across it?
