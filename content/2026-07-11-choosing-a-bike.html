Choosing a Bike

自行车 Bicycle身高 Height

父亲比较几款自行车，孩子决定买26寸自行车

Dad compares a few bikes, and the child decides on a 26-inch one

Speakers: A - 父亲 father | B - 孩子 child
A: 过来看一下
guò lái kàn yī xià
Come take a look.
要一起看电脑
They are going to look at the computer together.
B: 什么事 ？
shén me shì?
What is it?
A: 就不买24寸自行车了，买26寸的。
jiù bù mǎi 24 cùn zì xíng chē le, mǎi 26 cùn de.
Let's skip the 24-inch bike and get a 26-inch one.
A: 这是买给你最后一辆车了。如果后面还买只能你自己挣钱买了。
zhè shì mǎi gěi nǐ zuì hòu yī liàng chē le. rú guǒ hòu miàn hái mǎi zhǐ néng nǐ zì jǐ zhèng qián mǎi le.
This is the last bike we're buying you. If you want another one later, you'll have to earn the money and buy it yourself.
A: 迪卡侬26寸自行车，红色/蓝色，1米4就能骑。
dí kǎ nóng 26 cùn zì xíng chē, hóng sè / lán sè,1 mǐ 4 jiù néng qí.
Decathlon has a 26-inch bike in red or blue. You can ride it once you're 1.4 meters tall.
Decathlon EXPLORE 900，官方建议身高为140–160厘米。
Decathlon EXPLORE 900. Officially recommended for riders 140–160 cm tall.
A: 另外是捷安特这款，1米45就能骑。
lìng wài shì jié ān tè zhè kuǎn,1 mǐ 45 jiù néng qí.
Here's another one from Giant. You can ride it once you're 1.45 meters tall.
Giant ATX 660，适合身高145–160厘米。
Giant ATX 660. Suitable for riders 145–160 cm tall.
A: 如果你再等两年，这辆车到1米52才能骑，也是捷安特的。
rú guǒ nǐ zài děng liǎng nián, zhè liàng chē dào 1 mǐ 52 cái néng qí, yě shì jié ān tè de.
If you wait another two years, you can get this one. You need to be 1.52 meters tall to ride it. It's also a Giant.
Giant ATX 720，适合身高152–164厘米。
Giant ATX 720. Suitable for riders 152–164 cm tall.
B: 就买26寸的
jiù mǎi 26 cùn de
Let's get the 26-inch one.
B: 你等得越久，就可以买更好的。
nǐ děng dé yuè jiǔ, jiù kě yǐ mǎi gèng hǎo de.
If you wait longer, you can get a better one.
