98年的歌

A Song from 1998

音乐 Music年代 Generations

孩子发现《伤心太平洋》是 1998 年的歌，难以相信父亲听了二十多年

The child finds out "The Sad Pacific" came out in 1998 and can hardly believe Dad has been listening to it for over twenty years

Speakers: A - 父亲 father | B - 孩子 child
B: 你有听过《伤心太平洋》吗？
nǐ yǒu tīng guò 《 shāng xīn tài píng yáng 》 ma?
Have you ever heard "The Sad Pacific"?
母亲在 homepod 上放的歌，小孩不知道父亲以前什么时候听过？
Mom is playing the song on the HomePod, and the child does not know when Dad heard it before.
A: 都听了20年了。
dōu tīng le 20 nián le.
I've been listening to it for more than 20 years.
B: 不可能吧？
bù kě néng ba?
No way.
A: 我上网查一下时间。
wǒ shàng wǎng chá yī xià shí jiān.
Let me check online and see when it came out.
查歌曲发布时间。
He checks the song's release date.
B: 98年的！
98 nián de!
It came out in 1998!
孩子难以置信。
The child is surprised.
A: 那时都是听任贤齐、谢霆锋、周杰伦的歌。
nà shí dōu shì tīng rèn xián qí, xiè tíng fēng, zhōu jié lún de gē.
Back then, everyone listened to Richie Jen, Nicholas Tse, and Jay Chou.
