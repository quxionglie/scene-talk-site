“可爱”的表舅

The "Cute" Uncle

满月酒 Baby's one-month banquet可爱 Cute

满月酒后，孩子说表舅“有点可爱”？

After the banquet, it sounds like the child is calling their uncle "kind of cute."

Download PDF
A-父亲 fatherB-孩子 child
B: 今天去到几分钟就吃饭了，我吃了两碗饭。
jīn tiān qù dào jǐ fēn zhōng jiù chī fàn le, wǒ chī le liǎng wǎn fàn.
We'd only been there a few minutes when we started eating. I had two bowls of rice.
A: 那你们去到可能有点晚了。
nà nǐ men qù dào kě néng yǒu diǎn wǎn le.
Then you must've gotten there a little late.
B: 吃完饭，我们还在餐馆里跑来跑去。
chī wán fàn, wǒ men hái zài cān guǎn lǐ pǎo lái pǎo qù.
After we ate, we ran around the restaurant.
A: 其他人你都认识不？
qí tā rén nǐ dōu rèn shí bù?
Did you know everyone else there?
B: 有些认识，有些不认识。
yǒu xiē rèn shí, yǒu xiē bù rèn shí.
Some of them, but not all.
A: 你今天看到表舅了吗？
nǐ jīn tiān kàn dào biǎo jiù le ma?
Did you see your uncle today?
这里体现了中文和英文的差异。中文的"舅舅"、"叔叔"、"伯伯"、"姑父"、"姨父"、"表舅" 等 都可以翻译成"uncle"
This shows a difference between Chinese and English: "jiu jiu", "shu shu", "bo bo", "gu fu", "yi fu", and "biao jiu" are different kinship terms in Chinese, but they can all be translated as "uncle".
B: 看到了。表舅……有点可爱？
kàn dào le. biǎo jiù …… yǒu diǎn kě ài?
Yeah. My uncle's... kind of cute?
中文里很少用“可爱”这个词形容成年人。
In Chinese, adults aren't usually described as "cute."
A: 是表舅有点可爱吗？
shì biǎo jiù yǒu diǎn kě ài ma?
You mean your uncle was kind of cute?
父亲没听清孩子中间说的话，所以多问了一句。
Dad missed part of what the child said, so he asked again.
B: 表舅儿子有点可爱。我还摸了他...
biǎo jiù ér zi yǒu diǎn kě ài. wǒ hái mō le tā...
I meant my uncle's son. He was kind of cute. I even got to touch him...
