安全出口标志

The Emergency Exit Sign

地铁 Subway标志 Signs

坐地铁时孩子虽然不认识字，却认出了“安全出口”。

While riding the subway, the child recognizes the "Emergency Exit" sign even though they can't read yet.

Speakers: A - 父亲 father | B - 孩子 child
B: 那个标志是什么意思？
nà ge biāo zhì shì shén me yì si?
What does that sign mean?
A: 哪一个？
nǎ yí gè?
Which one?
B: 算了，说了反正你也不知道。
suàn le, shuō le fǎn zhèng nǐ yě bù zhī dào.
Never mind. You wouldn't know anyway.
A: 那门口旁边那个标志是什么意思？人在跑的那个!
nà mén kǒu páng biān nà ge biāo zhì shì shén me yì si? rén zài pǎo de nà ge!
Then what does that sign by the door mean? The one with the person running!
B: 安全出口。
ān quán chū kǒu.
Emergency exit.
A: 你不认识字也知道？
nǐ bù rèn shí zì yě zhī dào?
You know what it means even though you can't read yet?
惊讶的语气。
He sounds surprised.
B: 家里楼梯口也有一个“安全出口”。
jiā lǐ lóu tī kǒu yě yǒu yí gè “ān quán chū kǒu”.
There's an "Emergency Exit" sign by the stairs in our building too.
