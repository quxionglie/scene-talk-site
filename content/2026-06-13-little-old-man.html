一百岁的小老头

A Hundred-Year-Old Little Old Man

家庭 Family年龄 Age

父亲和孩子在家里聊年龄，孩子开玩笑说自己一百岁了

At home, a father and child talk about age, and the child jokes that they are one hundred years old

Speakers: A - 父亲 father|B - 孩子 child
A: 你今年多少岁了？
nǐ jīn nián duō shǎo suì le?
How old are you now?
B: 100岁了。
100 suì le.
I'm 100.
A: 那我能叫你为小老头吗？
nà wǒ néng jiào nǐ wèi xiǎo lǎo tóu ma?
Then can I call you a little old man?
B: 不行。
bù xíng.
No.
