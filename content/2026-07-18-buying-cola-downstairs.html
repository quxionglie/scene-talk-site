下楼买可乐

Going Downstairs to Buy Cola

购物 Shopping可乐 Cola

父亲让孩子下楼买两瓶可乐，学习如何买东西。

The father asks the child to go downstairs and buy two bottles of cola to practice shopping.

Speakers: A - 父亲 father | B - 孩子 child
A: 我给你 10 块钱，你去楼下给我买两瓶可乐，行不行？
wǒ gěi nǐ 10 kuài qián, nǐ qù lóu xià gěi wǒ mǎi liǎng píng kě lè, xíng bù xíng?
Here's 10 yuan. Can you go downstairs and buy me two bottles of cola?
B: 不行。
bù xíng.
No.
A: 是不会还是不行？
shì bú huì hái shì bù xíng?
Don't know how, or can't do it?
B: 我本来就不想买。
wǒ běn lái jiù bù xiǎng mǎi.
I just don't want to.
A: 你就去店里拿两瓶然后交给前台姐姐，后面再给钱就行了。
nǐ jiù qù diàn lǐ ná liǎng píng rán hòu jiāo gěi qián tái jiě jiě, hòu miàn zài gěi qián jiù xíng le.
Just grab two bottles, hand them to the lady at the counter, and then pay for them. That's it.
