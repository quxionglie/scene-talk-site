买玉米

Buying Corn

买菜 Grocery shopping付款 Payment

顾客在菜市场买两斤玉米

A customer buys two jin of corn at a market

Speakers: A - 顾客 customer|B - 叔叔 vendor
A: 叔叔，这个玉米多少钱一斤？
shū shū, zhè ge yù mǐ duō shǎo qián yī jīn?
Sir, how much is the corn per jin?
B: 2块5一斤。
2 kuài 5 yī jīn.
It's 2.5 yuan per jin
A: 帮我称2斤吧。
bāng wǒ chēng 2 jīn ba.
Two jin, please.
B: 称好了，一共5块钱。
chēng hǎo le, yī gòng 5 kuài qián.
Here you go. Five yuan total.
A: 好的，谢谢。
hǎo de, xiè xiè.
Okay, thanks.
