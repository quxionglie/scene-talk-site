葡萄盖饭

Grape Rice Bowl

家庭 Family食物 Food

孩子展示自己做的“葡萄盖饭”。

The child shows off a homemade “grape rice bowl.”

Speakers: A - 父亲 father | B - 孩子 child | C - 母亲 mother
B: 爸爸，来看我的葡萄盖饭。
bà bà, lái kàn wǒ de pú táo gài fàn.
Dad, come look at my grape rice bowl.
父亲都没听说过葡萄盖饭，显然是"奇怪食物"
The father has never heard of a grape rice bowl. It's obviously a “weird” dish.
A: 给你加点汤？
gěi nǐ jiā diǎn tāng?
Want me to pour some soup on it?
盖饭一般要浇点汤
Rice bowls usually come with sauce or broth over the rice.
B: 不要。
bú yào.
No!
C: 葡萄剥皮都花了 10 分钟。
pú táo bāo pí dōu huā le 10 fēn zhōng.
It took ten minutes just to peel the grapes.
孩子自己剥的。
The child peeled them all by themselves.
