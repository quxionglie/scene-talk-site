退休的老板

The Retired Boss

父亲节 Father's Day

父亲问孩子父亲节有没有礼物，孩子说老板已经退休了

A father asks his child if he will get a Father's Day gift, and the child says the boss has already retired

A-父亲 fatherB-孩子 child
A: 听说你今天又表演了节目？
tīng shuō nǐ jīn tiān yòu biǎo yǎn le jié mù?
I heard you put on another performance today?
幼儿园内部不定期会有节目，如六一儿童节班级舞蹈
The kindergarten sometimes puts on performances, such as class dances for Children's Day
B: 是啊，表演给爸爸的。
shì a, biǎo yǎn gěi bà bà de.
Yeah, it was for Dad.
A: 6月份父亲节，我还有礼物不？
6 yuè fèn fù qīn jié, wǒ hái yǒu lǐ wù bù?
Father's Day is in June. Do I get a gift too?
B: 没有。
méi yǒu.
No.
A: 你不是老板吗？
nǐ bú shì lǎo bǎn ma?
Aren't you the boss?
因为孩子过年有压岁钱，所以家里开玩笑叫孩子老板
The family jokingly calls the child the boss because he received lucky money during Lunar New Year
B: 我已经退休了。
wǒ yǐ jīng tuì xiū le.
I'm already retired.
A: 老板还会退休？
lǎo bǎn hái huì tuì xiū?
Bosses retire too?
