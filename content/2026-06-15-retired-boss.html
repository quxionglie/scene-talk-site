退休的老板

The Retired Boss

父亲节 Father's Day

父亲问孩子父亲节有没有礼物，家里被戏称为“老板”的孩子说自己已经退休了

Dad asks whether he'll get a Father's Day present. His child, whom the family jokingly calls "the boss," says he's already retired.

Speakers: A - 父亲 father|B - 孩子 child
A: 听说你今天又表演了节目？
tīng shuō nǐ jīn tiān yòu biǎo yǎn le jié mù?
I heard you performed again today.
B: 是啊，表演给爸爸的。
shì a, biǎo yǎn gěi bà bà de.
Yeah, I performed it for you.
A: 6月份父亲节，我还有礼物不？
6 yuè fèn fù qīn jié, wǒ hái yǒu lǐ wù bù?
Father's Day is coming up. Do I get a present too?
B: 没有。
méi yǒu.
No.
A: 你不是老板吗？
nǐ bú shì lǎo bǎn ma?
Aren't you the boss?
孩子过年收到压岁钱，有了自己的钱，所以家里开玩笑叫他“老板”
The family jokingly calls the child "the boss" because he received Lunar New Year lucky money and therefore has money of his own.
B: 我已经退休了。
wǒ yǐ jīng tuì xiū le.
I'm retired now.
A: 老板还会退休？
lǎo bǎn hái huì tuì xiū?
Bosses can retire too?
