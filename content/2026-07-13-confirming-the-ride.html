确认网约车

Confirming the Ride

网约车 Ride-hailing

网约车上车后司机确认乘客身份

After the passenger gets in, the driver confirms their identity

Speakers: A - 父亲 father|B - 孩子 child|C - 司机 driver
A: 我们的车快到了。
wǒ men de chē kuài dào le.
Our ride's almost here.
B: 哪俩车？
nǎ liǎ chē?
Which one?
A: 白色，2138 。
bái sè,2138.
The white one. The plate ends in 2138.
2138 是车牌尾号
2138 is the last four digits of the license plate number.
A: 那边那俩车，我们过去。
nà biān nà liǎ chē, wǒ men guò qù.
That one over there. Let's head over.
C: 是尾号 7629 吗？
shì wěi hào 7629 ma?
Does your phone number end in 7629?
坐好后，司机会通过手机尾号确认乘客身份，有时要你自己报号码。
Once you're seated, the driver checks the last four digits of your phone number to make sure you're the right passenger. Sometimes you may need to give the number first.
A: 是的。
shì de.
Yes.
可以接着说目的地，再次确认。如：是的，去万达广场。
You can also say where you're going to confirm the destination with the driver, for example, "Yes, we're going to Wanda Plaza."
