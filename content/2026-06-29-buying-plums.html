买李子

Buying Plums

买菜 Grocery shopping付款 Payment

顾客在菜市场买青李子和红李子

A customer buys green and red plums at a market

Download PDF
A-顾客 customerB-阿姨 vendor
A: 阿姨，这个李子怎么卖的？
ā yí, zhè ge lǐ zi zěn me mài de?
Auntie, how much are these plums?
B: 这种青色的3元一斤，红的4元一斤。
zhè zhǒng qīng sè de 3 yuán yī jīn, hóng de 4 yuán yī jīn.
The green ones are 3 yuan per jin. The red ones are 4 yuan per jin.
A: 这两种帮我各称一斤，一起7块吧。
zhè liǎng zhǒng bāng wǒ gè chēng yī jīn, yì qǐ 7 kuài ba.
One jin of each, please. That's 7 yuan total, right?
B: 好的，称好了。
hǎo de, chēng hǎo le.
Sure. Here you go.
A: 谢谢。
xiè xiè.
Thanks.
