快乐的歌

The Happy Song

音乐 Music听歌 Listening to music

孩子问“快乐的歌”是哪首，父亲才听出是在说五月天的《你不是真正的快乐》

The child asks about a "happy" song, and Dad realizes it is Mayday's "You Are Not Truly Happy"

Speakers: A - 父亲 father | B - 孩子 child
B: 昨天那首快……乐的歌是？
zuó tiān nà shǒu kuài …… lè de gē shì?
What was that "kuai... le" song from yesterday?
孩子说的是“快乐的歌”。
The child means "the happy song."
A: 唱得很快的歌？
chàng dé hěn kuài de gē?
A fast song?
当时正在用 HomePod 听歌，父亲没听清“乐”字。
They are listening to music on a HomePod, and the father only hears "kuai."
B: 快乐的歌。
kuài lè de gē.
The happy song.
A: 五月天，《你不是真正的快乐》。
wǔ yuè tiān,《 nǐ bú shì zhēn zhèng de kuài lè 》.
Mayday. "You Are Not Truly Happy."
A: 你不是真正的快乐……
nǐ bú shì zhēn zhèng de kuài lè ……
"Ni bu shi zhenzheng de kuaile..." (He sings a line from the song.)
父亲小唱了一句歌词。
The father sings a short lyric line.
