垃圾桶就是删除

The Trash Can Icon Means Delete

删除 Delete垃圾桶 Trash Can

孩子乱按手表时认出了表示删除的垃圾桶

While tapping around on his watch, the child realizes the trash can icon means delete

Speakers
A 父亲 father|B 孩子 child
B: 我要删除，这有垃圾桶。
wǒ yào shān chú, zhè yǒu lā jī tǒng.
I want to delete it. There's a trash can icon here.
乱按手表时，发现有垃圾桶图标。
He spots a trash can icon while tapping around on his watch.
A: 你怎么知道垃圾桶就是删除呢？
nǐ zěn me zhī dào lā jī tǒng jiù shì shān chú ne?
How do you know the trash can icon means delete?
B: 电视里有垃圾桶，我不知道是什么意思。按一下动画片就没了。
diàn shì lǐ yǒu lā jī tǒng, wǒ bù zhī dào shì shén me yì si. àn yī xià dòng huà piàn jiù méi le.
I saw a trash can icon on the TV. I didn't know what it meant, but when I pressed it, the cartoon disappeared.
按一下是指按下电视遥控器的确认键
Here, "press it" means pressing the OK button on the TV remote.
A: 这都被你发现了。
zhè dōu bèi nǐ fā xiàn le.
Wow, you even figured that out.
B: 还好删除的都不是我喜欢看的动画片。
hái hǎo shān chú de dōu bú shì wǒ xǐ huān kàn de dòng huà piàn.
Good thing I didn't delete any cartoons I like.
B: 小时候喜欢，现在不喜欢了。
xiǎo shí hòu xǐ huān, xiàn zài bù xǐ huān le.
I liked them when I was little, but not anymore.
